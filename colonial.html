Colonial and Western Culture

Structure we have inherited from colonial and western culture

Introduction

Colonialism impacted the African continent socially, politically and economically both positively and negatively.

Positive

Christianity

Christianity led to the demise of the African customs, which it viewed as pagan. The religion also led to the implementation of apartheid and undermined the leadership role of women.The Church has been a major source of social services like schooling and medical care, culture and philosophy and also play a role in politics and religion.

Education

English was considered as necessary for civilisation by the colonisers, which is why the British language policy was to switch the classroom language to English after the first years of schooling.

Negative

Slavery

  • chattel slavery
  • bonded labor
  • forced labor
